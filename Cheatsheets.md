## 💡 Pro-Tips for your Setup

* **Pane Naming:** In your current config, windows and panes will auto-rename based on the command you're running (like `nvim` or `zsh`). 
* **Floating Magic:** Don't forget `Prefix + f` for **Floax**. It's perfect for a quick calculator or a git command without losing your place in your code.
* **Neovim Synergy:** Because you have `vim-tmux-navigator`, you don't need the prefix to move between your code and your terminal—just use `Ctrl` + your movement keys.
* **Yazi Integration:** Type `y` in any pane to launch **Yazi**. With your `allow-passthrough` setting, image previews will work perfectly inside your Ghostty/Tmux combo.
